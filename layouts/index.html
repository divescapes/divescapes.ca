The Alberta Underwater Council is proud to present Divescapes 2020 Conference and Exhibition on Oct 23, 24, 2020 at the River Cree Resort & Casino in Edmonton, AB.

With world class presenters, exhibitors, workshops & prizes for the benefit & enjoyment of Alberta & beyond's scuba divers!

Divescapes 2020 Scuba Conference volunteers are busy now, creating another amazing Divescapes for your enlightenment and education.

Please SAVE THE DATE & join the Divescapes 2020 festivities and help us celebrate AUC's landmark 60th Anniversary and Divescapes' 25th Anniversary in 2020.

Divescapes Conference mandate includes striving to promote safe & environmentally responsible diving and provide all participants with the opportunity to partner with scuba industry organizations like participating local dive centres, government agencies, tourism, travel, clubs, service groups, etc., to develop a network of resources for underwater sports & scuba diving in Alberta & around the world.
